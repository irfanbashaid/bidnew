HOW IT WORKS?

  1. REGISTER AND CREATE AN ACCOUNT ON GRAABIT WEBSITE ENTER EMAIL ID, MOBILE NUMBER AND PASSWORD.
  2. CLICK ON SIGNUP, ONCE YOU’RE LOGGED IN TO YOUR YOU CAN BUY BID PACKS AND REGISTER FOR DEALS.
  3. ALL UPCOMING DEALS ARE SHOWN ON HOME PAGE AND DEALS PAGE.
  4. GO TO BUY DEAL BIDS PACKS AND BUY AS MANY BID PACKS AS YOU WISH.
  5. ONCE USER’S SELECTED THE PACK THEY CLICK ON PAY NOW USING ANY OF THE ONLINE PAYMENT METHODS LISTED ON WEBSITE.
  6. THE BIDS AND TOTAL BALANCE WILL BE SHOWN IN THE USER’S MY ACCOUNT PAGE.
  7. NEXT USERS GO TO UPCOMING DEALS PAGE AND THEN REGISTER FOR RESPECTIVE DEALS OF THEIR CHOICE USING MINIMUM REQUIRED BIDS FOR THE DEAL BEFORE THE DEAL AUCTION STARTS.
  8. WHEN REQUIRED NUMBER OF USERS REGISTER FOR THE DEAL AUCTION, REGISTRATION CLOSES. DEAL AUCTIONS START AT 8PM AND GO ON UNTIL 10 PM
  9. USERS CLICK ON BID NOW AND START PLACING THEIR BIDS ON THE PRODUCT EACH BID INCREASES THE PRICE BY SOME AMOUNT.
  10. A MAXIMUM OF 10 SECONDS ARE AVAILABLE FOR A USER TO PLACE THEIR NEXT BID.
  11. IF NO BIDS IS PLACED IN 10 SECONDS AUCTION ENDS AND LAST USER BID GRABS THE DEAL AT THAT PRICE.
  12. OR IF 2 HOURS ENDS AUCTION ENDS AND USER WITH HIGHEST BID WINS AND GRABS THE DEAL. WINNER GETS TO BUY THE PRODUCT AT WINNING BID PRICE.
  13. EVERYONE ELSE WHO PARTICIPATED IN THE DEAL GETS THE PRODUCT AT DEAL PRICE.
